Toddler

In our Pre-Montessori, children from 1.8 to 3 years can learn together. At Prodigy, children from this age group can learn from each other. This is the stage where kids will ask the question “why” as they develop their motor skills. We provide them with the best child-friendly environment where they can effectively learn motor and language skills. We encourage them to engage in group activities to develop their social skills and confidence. We help them become independent in performing their daily activities. At this stage, we mould them into bold individuals who can express themselves clearly with strong language and motor skills.

We have been a nurturing haven for young talents, where their uniqueness is recognized and celebrated.

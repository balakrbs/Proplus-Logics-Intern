About Prodigy

Welcome to Prodigy International Montessori School, where learning is a joyous adventure! Our name, “Prodigy,” derives from the Latin word “Prodigium,” which denotes a person or child with exceptional talent. Here at Prodigy, we believe that every child is extraordinary and deserving of special attention. Since our establishment on October 3rd, 2014, we have been a nurturing haven for young talents, where their uniqueness is recognized and celebrated

SCHOOL FACILITIES

ENGAGING & SPACIOUS SCHOOL CAMPUS

LIBRARY

Our children are allowed to visit the library at any time during the working hours for pleasure reading. At Prodigy, we believe that this will promote the life long love for reading.

SPORTS

The children have an opportunity to participate in various team and individual sports like football, cricket, badminton, basketball, archery etc.

SNACK

As per the nutritionist’s advice we provide healthy snack to our children.There is a snack corner in the environment and there is no time specified for snack.

SICK BAY

A staff nurse is employed to take care of the children when they fall sick.This room is equipped with the bed and first aid kit.

MONTESSORI-TRAINED ADULT

The trained montessori teacher links the child to activities and experiences in the prepared environment.

TRANSPORTATION

Our transport is equipped with the vehicle tracking system.The bus will pick-up and drop the students from their respective stops as per the school schedule.

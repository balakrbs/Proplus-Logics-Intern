Adolescents Program (12-18 years)

HOME / ADOLESCENTS PROGRAM (12-18 YEARS)

Montessori Education For
Adolescents

Maria Montessori emphasizes the significance of educating adolescents, as it is during this time that children transition into adulthood and become part of society. As teachers and parents, it is essential to provide them with the necessary care to navigate the intellectual, physical, and emotional changes they experience. By offering the right support and guidance, we can shape them into responsible citizens who contribute positively to society. At Prodigy, we hold guiding principles that aim to mould adolescents into both outstanding students and compassionate human beings. Our focus is on nurturing their growth and potential, helping them become well-rounded individuals ready to make a difference in the world.

Nurturing Responsible Adolescents - Embracing Growth and Empathy at Prodigy

At Prodigy, we deeply understand the significance of respecting and loving all adolescents as they navigate through challenging physical and emotional transformations. We recognize that during this period, they might be difficult to comprehend at times. Nevertheless, every adolescent who joins us is treated with utmost respect and care, as we aim to shape them into responsible individuals. This stage of life is marked by curiosity and a thirst for knowledge about the world around them. It is also a time where they may be vulnerable to making irresponsible choices. To guide them towards becoming responsible members of society, we engage them in meaningful activities that help them understand their roles and responsibilities within their community.

Empowering Adolescent Voices

Adolescents are full of ideas and questions, yearning to express themselves and be heard. At Prodigy, we provide a safe and open space for them to freely express their thoughts and feelings. We prioritize their intellectual growth, encouraging deep and critical thinking through our education approach. As they transition from a joyful childhood to a more aware phase, they may encounter challenges in the society. At Prodigy, we stand beside them, empowering them to face these challenges and emerge stronger. One significant challenge they encounter is understanding and embracing the bodily changes that come with adolescence. We offer them the support and guidance they need to accept and embrace these changes confidently. Our goal is to nurture adolescents with empathy, understanding, and a strong foundation to confidently embrace the journey towards adulthood.

Cultivating Social Responsibility - Empowering Adolescents for a Better Society at Prodigy

During adolescence, it is the perfect time to instill in kids the values of civility, a sense of community, and their role as responsible citizens in society. At Prodigy, we prioritize teaching these important lessons to our students. They not only learn about being good citizens but also discover how to be a considerate neighbour within their community. Our aim is to nurture them into becoming better human beings who actively serve and contribute positively to their community and society as a whole. Through our holistic approach to education, we empower adolescents to develop a strong sense of social responsibility, fostering a generation of compassionate and engaged individuals.

Empowering Adolescent Voices

During adolescence, individuals experience significant changes and heightened awareness about themselves and the world around them. At Prodigy, we support them through this transformative phase, helping them adjust to these new experiences. Our focus is on nurturing the development of their personal values system, which provides clarity about their identity and what they stand for in life. Through various engaging activities, we help them expand their vocabulary, enhancing their language skills for effective communication. Additionally, we encourage group activities to foster strong peer relationships. Our comprehensive approach ensures their intellectual, psychological, emotional, and physical growth, providing a stable foundation for their future endeavours. At Prodigy, we believe in guiding adolescents to become well-rounded individuals who are confident in expressing themselves and building positive connections with others.

Academic Programme

At Prodigy we have the following academic programme for Adolescents.

Our core subjects are Humanities (English, Social Science), Maths and Science.

Exploring the Human Story

The Journey of Humanities Education

In the world of education, the Humanities program stands as a beacon of knowledge and understanding about the human story. Through literature, history, and various activities, students embark on a journey of wisdom, self-expression, and critical thinking. At Prodigy, we place great importance on cultivating a passion for learning and igniting the spark of curiosity within each student. Let us now dwell into the various aspects of our Humanities program, emphasizing how it shapes students into well-rounded individuals ready to make a positive impact on the world.

Literature serves as a profound reflection of life, offering students insights into the joys, sorrows, and challenges faced by humanity. Through our core subject, “English,” students hone their writing, reading, and editing skills. Engaging exercises, such as writing letters, journals, essays, and poems, and researching various topics, foster creativity and effective communication. By mastering grammar, spelling, and vocabulary, students develop into excellent public speakers, equipped to express their thoughts confidently.

Studying history provides students with a glimpse into the lives of influential leaders and the revolutions they brought to society. This exposure inspires and instills leadership qualities, empowering students to envision themselves as change-makers. Our extensive history curriculum encompasses key events and movements that have shaped the world, empowering students to learn from the past and prepare for the future.

Through anthropology, students gain insights into contemporary social and cultural issues, fostering understanding and empathy. By exploring topics such as immigration, international development, religion, health, gender, and race, they develop a global perspective. Geography education allows students to learn about diverse places, their cultural nuances, and physical characteristics, making them more adept in an interconnected world.

In our dynamic world, staying informed about current events is essential. By incorporating current affairs into the curriculum, we nurture a habit of staying up-to-date and informed about global happenings. This knowledge becomes an integral part of students’ lifestyles, fostering a sense of responsibility towards the world. At Prodigy, our Humanities program serves as a gateway to understanding the human story and the world around us. Through literature, history, anthropology, geography, students grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. By empowering them with knowledge and fostering a passion for learning, we prepare them to become thoughtful, empathetic, and responsible global citizens, equipped to navigate the complexities of life with confidence and wisdom.

We have been a nurturing haven for young talents, where their uniqueness is recognized and celebrated.
